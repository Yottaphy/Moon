About Me

I am a Dual Doctoral Student, at the Universities of Liverpool (UK) and Jyväskylä (Finland). My PhD revolves around the MARA-LEB facility, in the accelerator laboratory at the Universtiy of Jyväskylä.
Here's a little timeline of my academic life:

  • 2020-

    Jyväskylä

    I moved to Finland in September 2020 to work at JYFL during the two middle years of my PhD.

  • 2019 - 2020

    Liverpool

    Started my PhD in Experimental Nuclear Physics at the University of Liverpool.

  • 2018 - 2019

    Master's Degree

    I completed a Master's Degree in Nuclear Physics. My degree is awarded by the University of Seville (Spain), but the degree was a joint degree, by the Universities of Barcelona, Complutense, Granada, Autónoma de Madrid, Salamanca and Sevilla, in addition to the Spanish Research Council (CSIC).

  • 2014 - 2018

    Bachelor Degree

    I completed a 4-year bachelor degree in Physics at the University of València (Spain).
